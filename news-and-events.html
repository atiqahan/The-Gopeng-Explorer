News & Events

Festivals & Events in Gopeng

Discover the vibrant events and festivals that make Gopeng an exciting destination for tourists all year round.

Festival of Lights - Space Science Park

Date: August to October 2025

A themed light festival with glowing space-themed installations like astronauts and rockets. Held at RTC Gopeng and great for night photography and family outings.

Radak Adventure White‑Water Rafting Weekend (Gopeng)

Date: August 2025

Hosted by Radak Adventure, this 3‑day weekend includes white‑water rafting, river tubing, jungle trekking, and riverside camping.

Tapaufest – Lenggong Outdoor Festival

Date: August 2025

Southeast Asia’s biggest indie-music and arts festival featuring 12 ASEAN artists, live bands, film screenings, artisan markets, kayaking, and yoga.
