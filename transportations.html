Transportation

Getting to Gopeng

Train (KTM Intercity)

Price: RM 4-16 from KL Sentral
Duration: 2h 22m from Kuala Lumpur
Schedule: Every 2 hours
Booking: KTMB website, app, or stations
Station: Gopeng Railway Station
Note: Scenic countryside, affordable

E-Hailing (Taxi / Others)

Price: RM 80-120 from KL
Duration: 2-2.5 hours
Booking: Apps (Grab, etc.)
Availability: 24/7
Pick-up: Door-to-door
Note: Most convenient, AC comfort

ETS (Electric Train Service)

Price: RM 15-35
Duration: ~2h total
Schedule: 5 services daily
Booking: KTMB online or app
Station: Ipoh (transfer needed)
Note: Fastest option, reserved seating

Bus

Price: RM 12-20 from KL
Duration: 2h 45m
Schedule: Frequent
Booking: Online or terminal
Terminal: Gopeng Bus Terminal
Note: Budget-friendly, AC comfort

Booking & Travel Information

Train Booking


Online: KTM Online Booking or KTMB Mobile App
Phone: +603 - 9779 1200 (Call Center)
Walk-in: Any KTM station counter
Payment: Credit/Debit card, online banking, cash
Booking Period: Up to 30 days in advance
Note: Book early for weekend/holiday travel

Bus Booking

Online: Online Ticket Bus
Walk-in: Terminal Bersepadu Selatan (TBS), Gopeng Bus Terminal
Payment: Credit card, FPX online banking, cash
Booking Period: Up to 30 days in advance
Companies: Perak Transit - For bus enquiry, kindly call 012-450 0483 or 012-450 0806, Plusliner - Customer Support 017-6592527, 017-3791143, 012-2527354, 012-5682527
Note: Check schedule changes during festive seasons

ETS (Electric Train Service)

Online: Online Booking or KTMB Mobile App
Walk-in: ETS counters at KL Sentral, Ipoh, and other major stations
Classes: Silver (basic), Gold (faster), Platinum (express)
Seating: Reserved, air-conditioned, onboard toilets
Booking Period: Up to 30 days in advance
Note: ETS is the fastest and most comfortable train option. Book early, especially for holidays or weekends.

E-Hailing Apps

Apps: Taxi, InDrive or SOCAR (car sharing)
Payment: Credit card, e-wallet, cash
Booking: Instant or advance booking
Vehicle Types: Taxi, GrabCar, GrabCar Plus, GrabFamily
Features: GPS tracking, driver details, fare estimate
Note: Limited availability in rural areas

Important Travel Tips

Best Travel Times

  • Weekdays: Less crowded, better prices
  • Avoid school holidays & festive seasons
  • Early morning departures recommended
  • Check weather conditions for outdoor activities

Budget Planning

  • Bus: Most economical (RM 12-20)
  • Train: Good value (RM 4-16)
  • ETS: Premium comfort (RM 15-35)
  • Car: Flexible but higher cost (RM 150-250/day total)
  • E-hailing: Convenient but expensive (RM 80-120)

What to Bring

  • Valid identification (IC/Passport)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Light jacket for air-conditioned transport
  • Portable charger for mobile devices
  • Cash for local purchases

Local Transportation

  • Gopeng town is walkable
  • Grab available for attractions
  • Local taxis: RM 10-20 for short trips
  • Bicycle rental: RM 15-25/day
  • Tour packages include transport
