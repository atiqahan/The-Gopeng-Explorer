About

Background

Gopeng is a small town in Perak known for its rich heritage, lush rainforests, limestone caves, and welcoming village community. It offers a unique blend of adventure and culture, making it an unforgettable destination in Malaysia.

Gopeng town is located about 20 kilometers from the capital of Perak, Ipoh, and Gopeng is actually a quite strategic location because it is surrounded by big cities. In addition to Ipoh, Gopeng town is also in the Kampar city circle which is in the South and Batu Gajah (West). Due to its strategic location, the situation makes Gopeng town unable to escape from the daily routine of the people around the area. In Gopeng there are also limestone caves that are safe to explore, namely Gua Tempurung and Gua Kandu.

It is said that the name of the town of Gopeng was given based on the name of a famous individual in the area at a time when tin mining activities were actively carried out. The name of the individual was none other than Goh Peng. Goh Peng was a miner from China who came to Malaya following a famine and major flood disaster in China at a time in the past.

The presence of Goh Peng in the area known as Pekan Gopeng today has opened a new chapter in the development pattern of the area. Before the name Gopeng town came into being, the area was better known as Gunung Mesah.
